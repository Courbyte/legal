1. Introduction

Welcome to Courbyte Arena's Privacy Policy. Courbyte Arena operates an AI-powered quiz platform that instantly converts documents, PDFs, images, and video URLs into interactive quizzes. Our platform provides educational resources ranging from primary school entrance to master's level, including access to thousands of past questions and practice tests (JAMB, JUPEB, POST-UTME, GMAT, and 30+ more), learning programming using our live code editor, and access to free developer tools.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and disclose your information, including personal information, in connection with your access to and use of our platform.

Note: By using our services, you consent to the collection, transfer, storage, disclosure, and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our platform:

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, gender types,date of birth.
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, biography, educational interests
  • Contact Information: Email address, whatsapp number (optional)
  • Billing Information: Payment details for premium services (handled by secure payment processors)

2.2 Educational Data

  • Quiz results, scores, and performance analytics
  • Learning progress and course completion data
  • Practice test attempts and results
  • Code editor usage and programming exercises
  • Uploaded documents, PDFs, images, and video URLs for quiz generation

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device information (hardware model, operating system)
  • Browser type and version
  • IP address and approximate location data
  • Usage data and service logs

AI Processing Note: When you upload documents for quiz generation, our AI processes the content to create interactive quizzes. This content is processed securely and may be stored temporarily to improve our AI algorithms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve our AI-powered quiz platform and educational services
  • Personalization: To personalize your learning experience and recommend relevant content
  • AI Processing: To process uploaded documents and generate interactive quizzes
  • Progress Tracking: To track your learning progress and provide performance analytics
  • Communication: To send you service-related notices, updates, and educational content
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and ensure platform security
  • Research & Development: To improve our AI algorithms and educational offerings
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and educational standards

Educational Purpose: All data collection and processing serves the primary purpose of enhancing your educational experience and providing effective learning tools.

4. IP Addresses and Location Data

We collect and use IP addresses for the following purposes:

4.1 Approximate Location Detection

We may use your IP address to determine your approximate geographic location (city/country level) for:

  • Personalization: To customize educational content based on regional curriculum requirements
  • Localized Testing: To provide relevant practice tests (e.g., JAMB for Nigeria, GMAT for international users)
  • Compliance: To ensure we comply with regional data protection regulations
  • Service Optimization: To route your requests to the nearest server for faster performance

4.2 How We Use Location Data

  • To recommend educational content relevant to your region
  • To provide appropriate language and currency settings
  • To comply with regional educational standards and testing requirements
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activities

Accuracy Note: IP-based location detection provides approximate location only (typically accurate to city level). We do not collect precise GPS location data unless explicitly permitted by you for specific features.

Important: We do not use IP addresses or location data to personally identify you, except as necessary for security purposes or as required by law.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

5.1 With Your Consent

We will share your personal information with third parties when we have your consent to do so.

5.2 Service Providers

We may share information with third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf, such as:

  • Payment processing services
  • Cloud hosting and storage providers
  • Analytics and performance monitoring services
  • AI and machine learning service providers

5.3 Educational Institutions

If you use our platform through an educational institution, we may share progress reports and performance data with your institution as part of their educational services.

5.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

Data Protection: All third-party service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure coding practices and regular updates
  • Employee training on data protection

Despite our efforts, no security measures are completely impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

AI Security: Uploaded documents for AI quiz generation are processed in secure environments with strict access controls. Content is automatically deleted after processing unless retained for service improvement (with anonymization).

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing of your information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your information to another service
  • Objection: Object to processing of your information
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Educational Data: Note that certain educational data may be retained for academic integrity and historical record-keeping purposes, as permitted by law.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login information
  • Understand how you use our platform
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Analyze platform performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our platform.

Essential Cookies: Some cookies are essential for the platform to function properly and cannot be disabled.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for educational purposes and may be used by children under 13 with parental consent. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

For children under 13, we:

  • Require verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information
  • Collect only the minimum necessary information
  • Allow parents to review, delete, and refuse further collection of their child's information
  • Do not condition participation on providing more information than reasonably necessary

Parental Control: Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal information without consent should contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

We will also provide email notification of material changes to the Privacy Policy. Your continued use of the platform after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Review Regularly: We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Questions About Privacy?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please don't hesitate to contact our privacy team.
